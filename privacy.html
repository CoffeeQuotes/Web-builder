Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

CQ Web Builder is primarily a client-side application. The code you write inside the editor is processed entirely within your browser and saved to your device's local storage. We do not transmit, read, or store your code on our servers.

However, when you visit our website, standard connection data automatically recorded by hosting providers is collected, including your IP address, browser type, and time of visit.

2. Cookies and Web Beacons

We use cookies to store your preferences, such as editor themes and settings, ensuring a consistent experience across sessions.

We also use third-party advertisements on CQ Web Builder to support our site. Some of these advertisers (like Google AdSense) may use technology such as cookies and web beacons, which means they may collect information including your IP address, ISP, and browser information. This is generally used for geotargeting purposes or showing personalized ads based on specific sites visited.

3. Google AdSense & the DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google, as a third party advertisement vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on this website. The use of DART cookies by Google enables them to serve tailored adverts to visitors based on their visits to this website and other sites on the internet.

To opt out of the DART cookies you may visit the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL: http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html

Tracking of users through the DART cookie mechanisms are subject to Google's own privacy policies.

4. Your Consent

By using our site, you consent to our website's Privacy Policy. If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page.
